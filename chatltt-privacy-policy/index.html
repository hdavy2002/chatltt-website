  • ChatLTT Privacy Policy

At ChatLTT, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our users’ personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

1. Information Collection

  • Personal Information: We do not collect personally identifiable information from users unless voluntarily provided to us for the purpose of creating an account, accessing certain features, or contacting us for support.
  • Non-Personal Information: We may collect non-personal information automatically as you navigate through our website or use our services. This information may include usage details, device information, and other technical data.

2. Use of Information

  • We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, as well as to communicate with users, respond to inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • We do not use customers’ data to retrain our AI models or share any data with third parties for such purposes.

3. Data Security

  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard the security and confidentiality of users’ data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Retention

  • We retain user data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Our services may include links to third-party websites, plugins, or services that are not controlled by ChatLTT. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

  • Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

  • We reserve the right to update or revise this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
