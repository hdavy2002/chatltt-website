Commission Structure and Income Projection:

  • Commission Structure and Income Projection:

Dear Influencers,

We are thrilled to have you join the ChatLTT platform, where you have the opportunity to engage with your audience in exciting and innovative ways. As you embark on your journey with us, we want to ensure transparency and clarity regarding our commission structure.

Standard and Uncensored Instances:

At ChatLTT, we offer two types of instances: Standard and Uncensored.

  • Standard Instances: These instances do not entertain sensitive adult conversations. Content such as sexual intimacy or explicit topics will be flagged, and our AI will not engage in such discussions.
  • Uncensored Instances: In this instance, influencers have the freedom to train the AI to speak about anything. While there are policies in place prohibiting engagement in topics like child pornography, violence, etc., influencers have more flexibility in the conversations they can have.

Commission Structure:

  1. Standard Instance:
  • Fan Subscription Fee: USD 2
  • Platform Fee: USD 1.19
  • Commission to Influencers: Rest of the amount
  1. Uncensored Instance:
  • Fan Subscription Fee: USD 3
  • Platform Fee: USD 2.19
  • Commission to Influencers: Rest of the amount

Withdrawal Process:

  • Minimum Withdrawal: USD 100
  • Once your account hits the minimum withdrawal threshold, you can request withdrawal through your ‘earnings dashboard’ on our platform.
  • Our team will review your request, and upon approval, the funds will be wired to your PayPal account.
  • For any billing-related queries or issues, please contact [email protected].

Additional Information:

  • Our platform fees include charges such as Stripe fees, Stripe Radar, PayPal transfer fees, etc.
  • To access your earnings dashboard and request withdrawals, visit ChatLTT Earnings Dashboard.

We are committed to supporting you in your journey as influencers on ChatLTT. Should you have any questions or require further assistance, please don’t hesitate to reach out to us.

Income Projection

Understanding your potential earnings on ChatLTT is crucial for planning and strategizing your content and promotional efforts. Here’s a breakdown of how you can calculate your monthly income based on user subscriptions:

  1. Monthly Income Calculation:
  • For every user subscription, you earn a commission based on the difference between the fan subscription fee and the platform fee.
  • Let’s assume:
    • Fan Subscription Fee: USD 2 (Standard Instance) or USD 3 (Uncensored Instance)
    • Platform Fee: USD 1.19 (Standard Instance) or USD 2.19 (Uncensored Instance)
    • Your Commission: Difference between fan subscription fee and platform fee
  1. Income Projection:
  • Suppose 5,000 users sign up:
    • Monthly Income = (Fan Subscription Fee – Platform Fee) * Number of Users
    • For example, with 5,000 users:
    • Standard Instance: Monthly Income = (2 – 1.19) * 5000 = USD 0.81 * 5000 = USD 4,050
    • Uncensored Instance: Monthly Income = (3 – 2.19) * 5000 = USD 0.81 * 5000 = USD 4,050
  • Similarly, if 100,000 users subscribe:
    • Standard Instance: Monthly Income = (2 – 1.19) * 100,000
    • Uncensored Instance: Monthly Income = (3 – 2.19) * 100,000
  1. User Fluctuations:
  • It’s important to note that user subscriptions may fluctuate over time due to factors such as failed credit card transactions, unsubscribes, etc.
  • You can monitor the details of your monthly orders and subscriptions on your earnings dashboard.
  1. Promotion Strategy:
  • Promote your ChatLTT page URL (e.g., https://chatjipt.co/listing/dr-education-faq/) to your audience to encourage subscriptions.
  • Engage with your audience and highlight the unique features and benefits of subscribing to your AI on ChatLTT.

Understanding your potential income can help you set realistic goals and optimize your efforts to maximize your earnings on ChatLTT. Don’t hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or need further assistance.
